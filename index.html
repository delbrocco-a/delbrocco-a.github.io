My Blog

We all have hobbies, they make up the things that we do everyday. The difference is, that we don't always feel proud of what we do, or we think that the daily things that make up our everday lives, somehow don't warrent any worthwhile time talking about. How have we convicned ourselves that the mundane everyday things that we do can't be fun, and that what most of our time and effort goes into our everyday lives, doesn't count as living a life that we want to love?

My Holidays

Welcome to my personal page! This serves as essentially a online public diary, where I document the times in my life that I feel are really worth talking about. This ranges from my holidays and hobbies, to my personal blog, where I dictate about what is on my mind, and some of the experiences, memories and interactions I've had, which give me the urge to write down the compulsive thoughts in my head, to hopefully give myself a better understanding of them, but also to document them, and show them as part of who I am. Hence, I apologise in advance, if in any of these parts if I come off as vain, or self-centred, as I feel as that's almost the nature of this kind of online content: simply talking about oneself.
